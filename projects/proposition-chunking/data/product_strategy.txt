Athlete Services Charter
------------------------
1. Every elite training center receives weekly surface scans that quantify wear, energy return, and safety hazards.
2. For long-form content, coaches expect propositions that map inputs to verifiable claims so that retrieval remains truthful.
3. When a model cannot find a grounded answer it must say so, log the query, and trigger an annotation task.
4. The data team catalogs every apparel fabric experiment with propositions describing composition, purpose, and season.

Proposition-oriented chunking helps capture these facts. Each proposition is a concise, testable statement that stands on its own.
